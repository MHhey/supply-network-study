Supplier Selection Research Study

Online behavioral decision task • approx. 15–30 minutes

Purpose

This study examines how different information displays (e.g., transparency, reputation, verification) affect supplier selection and trust calibration in construction supply chains.

What you’ll do

  • Review supplier and 3PL profiles in short, simulated “weeks.”
  • Choose a supplier each week and rate your confidence.
  • Progress through several information scenarios (baseline, transparency, reputation, verification).

Time

Most participants complete the task in about 15–30 minutes. You may stop at any time by closing the browser.

Risks & benefits

Risks: Minimal; similar to typical online surveys/decision tasks.

Benefits: No direct personal benefit. Findings will help researchers understand how professionals evaluate suppliers under different information conditions.

Data & privacy

  • We record your choices, timing, and interaction events (e.g., clicks on supplier nodes).
  • No personal identifiers are collected; data are analyzed in aggregate.
  • Data are stored securely and used for research purposes only.
