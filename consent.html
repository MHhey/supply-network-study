Supplier Selection Research Study

Virginia Tech IRB #25-1022 • 15–30 minutes • Online behavioral decision task

Purpose

This study examines how different information displays—transparency, reputation, and verification—affect supplier-selection decisions and trust calibration in construction supply chains.

What you’ll do

  • Review supplier/3PL profiles across four short “weeks” per scenario.
  • Select an option each week and rate your confidence.
  • Total time about 15–30 minutes.

Risks & benefits

Minimal risk. Participation is voluntary and may be ended at any time. No direct personal benefit.

Data & privacy

  • We record your choices, clicks, timing, and confidence ratings only.
  • No personal identifiers are collected; data are analyzed in aggregate.
  • Data are stored securely on Virginia Tech servers.
