Supplier Selection Research Study

Online decision-making task • approximately 15-30 minutes

Purpose

This study examines how different information displays, such as transparency, reputation, and verification, influence supplier selection and trust in construction supply chains.

What you’ll do

You will play the role of a contractor or buyer making procurement decisions within a simulated supply network.

  • Review supplier and Third-Party Logistics (3PL) profiles that simulate four consecutive project weeks.
  • Choose which supplier to work with each round and rate your confidence in that choice.
  • The four weeks and scenarios happen back-to-back in one gameplay session (about 15-30 minutes), and the information within each scenario remains the same throughout those rounds.
  • There are no right or wrong answers, we’re interested in how you make decisions.

Time

The study typically takes about 15-30 minutes. You may stop at any time by closing the browser window.

Data and privacy

  • Your choices, timing, and interactions (for example, clicks on supplier nodes) will be recorded.
  • No personal identifiers are collected; data are analyzed only in aggregate.
  • All information is stored securely and used only for research purposes.
